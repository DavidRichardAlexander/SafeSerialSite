About Safe Serial

We make transactions for luxury goods safer by moving certificates of authenticity online. Our platform makes it easy to manage these items with simple ways to add or transfer them.

Create an Account

Provide us with some basic information and setup your account. You are then able to add and exchange items for free.

Add an Item

To add an item simply enter the brand, serial number, and transfer code to have any item added to your account in seconds.

Transfer Ownership

When you're ready to transfer ownership all you need to do is create a transfer code and provide it to the recipient.
